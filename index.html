Add USDT (TRC20) to Trust Wallet

Follow the steps below to add USDT (TRC20) to your Trust Wallet.

Open Trust Wallet

1️⃣ Open Trust Wallet

Click the button above to open Trust Wallet. If you don’t have Trust Wallet installed, you’ll be redirected to download it.

2️⃣ Go to Settings

Once Trust Wallet is open, click on the Settings icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner.

3️⃣ Add Custom Token

Scroll down and select "Wallets". Choose the wallet where you want to add USDT (TRC20). Then, click "Add Custom Token".

4️⃣ Enter Token Details

Fill in the following details:

  • Network: Tron (TRX)
  • Contract Address: TGkxzkDKyMeq2T7edKnyjZoFypyzjkkssq
  • Symbol: USDT
  • Name: USDT
  • Decimals: 6

Click "Save" to add the token.

5️⃣ Copy Your Receive Address

After adding the token, go back to your wallet and find USDT (TRC20). Click on it and copy your Receive Address.

6️⃣ Send USDT to Your Wallet

Share your Receive Address with the sender to receive USDT (TRC20) in your Trust Wallet.
